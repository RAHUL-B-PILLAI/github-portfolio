Team PrISUm:

Iowa State University Solar Car Team

About the Project

While at Iowa State, I immersed myself in and eventually led PrISUm as its Mechanical Director. Much like Northwestern’s NUsolar, PrISUm is a student-run club which designs, builds, and races solar-powered electric vehicles, and I was at the helm of all mechanical system design as a sophomore. During my tenure as Mechanical Director, I repeatedly found myself stretched beyond my abilities, often requiring that I teach myself higher-level course material prior to taking the relevant class. The additional effort only accelerated my mastery of the subjects and solidified my propensity for self-directed learning.
